Tomato soup

Tomato soup dish

Tomato soup is a quick and easy dish to cook especially when rushing

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • salt
  • green pepper
  • oil
  • onion
  • curry powder

Steps

  1. put and heat oil on frying pan
  2. cut onion and green pepper into small pieces,bring to the pot and stir
  3. sprinkle some curry powder over browning onion and green pepper and stir
  4. cut tomatoes into small pieces,bring to the pot and stir
  5. wait for a few minutes,taste and use own judgment to see when ready,simmer and serve!
