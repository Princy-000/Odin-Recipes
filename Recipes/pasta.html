A chicken curry Dish

Chicken curry is a universal dish loved by most people

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • oil
  • onion
  • curry powder
  • chicken soup
  • Rice
  • salt

Steps

  1. put oil on frying pan
  2. put onion and fry until brown
  3. add curry powder and stir
  4. add chicken portions and stir
  5. add half a teaspoon salt and stir
  6. mix chicken soup seperately in a cup,bring to the pot and stir
  7. wait and stir for a few minutes then simmer
  8. on a different pot put rice and water,let to boil for 30 minutes then drain water,simmer,then serve together with stew
