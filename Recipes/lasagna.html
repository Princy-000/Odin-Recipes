A lasagna Dish

lasagne is an italian dish that is created in italy and is delicious

Ingredients

  • oil
  • cheese
  • lasagna spice for souce
  • onion
  • Bacon
  • Curry Powder
  • mince

Steps

  1. Put oil on frying pan and start heating
  2. Chop Onion and add to frying pan
  3. fry the onion until slightly brown
  4. add curry powder and stir
  5. add mince,bacon and stir
  6. mix lasagna souce seperate in a cup then add to the pan and stir
  7. finally,great some cheese in to the pan and stir
  8. Use own judgement and taste to see when it is ready!let it simmer and serve
